📄 Privacy Policy

What we collect: We collect only the necessary information you provide when booking a job (e.g., Facebook username, location, availability, etc.).

How we use it: Your data is only used for booking purposes and to contact you about your request. It is never shared or sold to anyone.

Data retention: Booking information is stored securely and may be retained for reference. You may request to have it removed anytime.

Contact: For questions about your data, reach out to Reuben through Messenger or the booking email provided.

📄 Terms of Service

Booking: By booking a service, you agree to provide accurate information and be available at the agreed-upon time.

Rescheduling: You may reschedule up to 24 hours before your booking by contacting Reuben directly.

Payment: Payments can be made via cash or bank transfer after services are completed. You may choose how much to pay based on the suggested price or your own fair offer.

Safety: Please disclose any safety concerns in advance, especially regarding pets or access to your property.

Right to refuse: Reuben reserves the right to refuse or cancel a booking at any time for safety or scheduling conflicts.
